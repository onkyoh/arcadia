Flexible Paths to Ownership

Funding and financing options may vary depending on your intended use, property type, location, structure model, and ownership arrangement.

Potential pathways may include traditional financing, personal property investment, revenue-generating models, partnership structures, and phased deployment options. The goal is to create flexible solutions that fit your vision and goals.

Can These Generate Income?

In the right location, short-term rental demand can create strong income potential. Some owners may choose personal use only, part-time Airbnb use, or full-time income generation.

Depending on the setup and agreement structure, revenue-sharing or land-lease models may also be explored. Examples may include situations where the property owner provides the location, Arcadia provides the structure, and revenue is shared based on agreement terms.

Every property and partnership structure would be evaluated individually.
